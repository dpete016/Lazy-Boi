### About

Lazyboi Media
Are you lazy, just want to chill & listen to your favorite tracks? Let us do the leg work!

- Search our extensive library of music
- Select the tracks that fit your vibe
- Add tracks to personal playlists
- Access your playlists anytime, lazyBoi

### How to Use
1. Create an account with your username and password in Sign Up.

2. Explore thousands of varities of soundtracks, artists, genres. What ever your lazy heart desires.

3. Find & select your favorite tracks that you're vibing with the most.

4. Add your songs to a personal playlist to be accessed anytime you want
